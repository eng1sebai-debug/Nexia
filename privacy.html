Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Nexia, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We will never sell your data to anyone.

Section 01

Who We Are

Nexia is an AI-powered lead qualification platform designed for real estate agencies. We help agencies capture, qualify, and manage leads automatically through AI agents connected to Telegram and Google Sheets.

Questions? Contact us at support@nexia.ai

Section 02

What Data We Collect

When a lead interacts with our AI agent, we may collect:

  • Full name or anonymous interaction status
  • Phone number (including country code)
  • Email address
  • City or neighborhood of interest
  • Property type (Apartment, Villa, Office, Land)
  • Budget or expected sale price
  • Purchase or sale timeline
  • Intent type (Buyer or Seller)

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond the above.

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

  • To qualify and categorize real estate leads
  • To notify the real estate agency via Telegram
  • To store lead information in Google Sheets
  • To enable the agency to follow up with leads
  • To improve our AI agent's performance

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond connecting you with a real estate specialist.

Section 04

Who We Share Data With

  • The real estate agency you contacted
  • Google Sheets — secure data storage
  • Telegram — instant notifications
  • Voiceflow — AI conversation platform
  • Make.com — automation platform

We never sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party.

Section 05

Data Retention

Lead data is retained for a maximum of 12 months. After this period, data is permanently deleted from all systems. You may request deletion at any time.

Section 06

Your Rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to object to data use
  • Right to data portability
Section 07

Security

All data is encrypted via HTTPS. Our third-party providers maintain enterprise-grade security. Our website is a static site with no backend database, significantly reducing breach risk.

Section 08

Cookies

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Only essential cookies may be used to ensure the website functions correctly.

Section 09

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
