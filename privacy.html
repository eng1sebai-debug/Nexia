Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Nexia, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We will never sell your data to anyone.

Section 01

Who We Are

Nexia is an AI-powered lead qualification platform designed specifically for real estate agencies. We help agencies capture, qualify, and manage leads automatically through AI agents connected to Telegram and Google Sheets.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at: support@nexia.ai

Section 02

What Data We Collect

When a lead interacts with our AI agent, we collect the following information (if provided by the user):

  • Full name or interaction status (e.g., Anonymous Appointment)
  • Phone number (including country code)
  • Email address
  • City or neighborhood of interest
  • Property type (Apartment, Villa, Office, Land)
  • Budget or expected sale price
  • Purchase or sale timeline
  • Intent type (Buyer or Seller)

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To qualify and categorize real estate leads
  • To notify the relevant real estate agency via Telegram
  • To store lead information in the agency's Google Sheets
  • To enable the agency to follow up with the lead
  • To improve the AI agent's performance over time

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond connecting you with a real estate specialist.

Section 04

Who We Share Data With

Your data is shared only with the following trusted parties:

  • The real estate agency you contacted — to follow up on your inquiry
  • Google Sheets — for secure data storage
  • Telegram — for instant agency notifications
  • Voiceflow — our AI conversation platform
  • Make.com — our automation platform

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.

Section 05

How Long We Keep Your Data

Lead data is retained for a maximum of 12 months from the date of collection. After this period, data is permanently deleted from all systems.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

Section 06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to correction — request we fix inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion — request we delete your data
  • Right to object — object to how we use your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Section 07

Security

We take data security seriously. All data transmitted through our platform is encrypted using HTTPS. Our third-party providers (Google, Telegram, Voiceflow, Make.com) maintain their own enterprise-grade security standards.

Our website is a static site with no backend database, which significantly reduces the risk of data breaches.

Section 08

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We may use essential cookies only to ensure the website functions correctly.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
